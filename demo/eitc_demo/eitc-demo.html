About the Earned Income Tax Credit (EITC)

The EITC is a refundable tax credit for low to moderate income workers. This tool helps you determine if you're eligible based on 2024 tax year rules.

Note: this simplified demo excludes checks for improper claims history, qualifying child tie breaker rules, etc.
